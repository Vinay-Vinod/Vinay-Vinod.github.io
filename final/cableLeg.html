Squat 1. Start by standing with your feet shoulder-width apart
2. Keeping your back straight and your core engaged, slowly lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back, until your thighs are parallel to the ground.
3. Pause for a second at the bottom, then push through your heels to stand back up to the starting position. Repeat for desired number of repetitions.
Lunge 1. Start by standing with your feet shoulder-width apart, with the lunge resting on the upper portion of your hip.
2. Take a step forward with your right foot, bending both knees to lower into a lunge position, with your left knee hovering above the ground.
3. Push back up through your right heel to stand back up, then repeat on the other side, stepping forward with your left foot and alternating between legs for multiple reps.
