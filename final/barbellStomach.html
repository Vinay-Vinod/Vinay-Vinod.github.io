BARBELL STOMACH
Barbell Rollout 1.Kneel on the floor and grip a loaded barbell with an overhand grip, shoulder-width apart. Roll the bar forward by extending your arms, while keeping your back straight and core tight.
2.Continue to roll the bar forward until your arms are fully extended and your body is in a straight line.
3. Repeat for your desired number of reps, being sure to keep your core engaged throughout the exercise to prevent your back from sagging.
Single-Arm Landmine Press 1. Start with a landmine set up in a corner or landmine attachment on a barbell, with one end of the barbell placed in the corner or attachment.
2.Press the barbell upward and away from your body, extending your arm fully while keeping your torso stable. Then lower the barbell back down to your shoulder in a controlled manner.
3. Complete the desired number of reps on one side, then switch sides and repeat the movement.
