BARBELL SHOULDER
Shoulder Press 1. Set up the barbell at a comfortable weight on a squat rack or power rack at shoulder height
2. Lift the barbell off the rack, take a step back, and position your feet directly under your hips. Brace your core, keep your elbows pointed forward, and lower the barbell to your shoulders.
3. Press the barbell upward, extending your arms overhead until the barbell is directly above your shoulders.
Front Raise 1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and grasp a barbell with an overhand grip, resting the barbell on your thighs.
2.Slowly raise the barbell up to shoulder height, keeping your arms straight and your elbows slightly bent.
3. Lower the barbell back down to your thighs in a controlled manner, and repeat for desired number of repetitions.
