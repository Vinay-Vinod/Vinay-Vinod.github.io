CABLE ARM
Bicep Curl 1. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and grasp the cable with an underhand grip, hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
2.Keeping your elbows close to your sides, curl the cable up towards your shoulders while contracting your biceps. Pause briefly at the top of the movement.
3. Slowly lower the cable back down to the starting position and repeat for the desired number of repetitions.
Skull Crusher 1. Lie on a bench and hold a cable with an overhand grip, arms extended straight up over your chest.
2. Keeping your upper arms stationary, lower the cable towards your forehead by bending your elbows, making sure to keep your elbows in line with your shoulders.
3. Pause for a second at the bottom of the movement and then extend your arms back to the starting position, squeezing your triceps at the top. Repeat for desired reps.
