Bench Press 1. Lie down on the bench with your feet firmly on the ground and your back flat against the bench.
2.Grasp the bar with a grip slightly wider than shoulder width and lift it off the rack, keeping your arms straight and your elbows locked.
3. Lower the bar down to your chest, bending your elbows and keeping your wrists straight, then push the bar back up to the starting position. Repeat for desired number of repetitions.
Decline Bench Press 1. Lie down on a decline bench with your feet secure and grip the barbell with an overhand grip slightly wider than shoulder-width apart.
2.Lower the bar to your chest in a controlled manner, keeping your elbows tucked in and your back flat.
3. Push the bar back up to starting position while exhaling, keeping your core tight and maintaining proper form throughout the movement.
